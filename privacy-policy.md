## ENGLISH

**HT Studio 987** ("we", "our") respects the privacy of the users of **abc987** ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use our app.

### 1. What Data Do We Collect?
Our App **does not request or collect any personal data** (real name, phone number, address, email, precise location, IP address, etc.).

The App only temporarily processes the following **anonymous** data required for the game to function:
* **Nickname:** A random name you choose to enter the game. Please **do not** use your real name or sensitive personal information as a nickname.

* **Game Scores & Words:** Points and word lists generated during gameplay.

### 2. How Is Data Used?
This minimal data is used solely for:
* Providing the multiplayer gaming experience.
* Allowing players in the same room to see each other's scores.

This data is **never** used for advertising, marketing, or tracking purposes and is not shared with third parties for commercial use.

Our App uses **Google Firebase** infrastructure. Your data is hosted on Google's secure servers.
* **Automatic Deletion:** All data generated after a game (scores, nicknames, words) is kept on our servers for a maximum of **7 days**. After 7 days, it is **automatically and permanently deleted** by the system.

Any "Support the Developer" (Donation) transactions within the App are processed securely entirely through the **Google Play Store**. We never have access to, nor do we store, your credit card information or payment details.
